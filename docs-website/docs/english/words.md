# English Words

## Bring

bring is used to **carry someting** or **come with it** or **take something to a place**

+ Please bring your laptop to the meeting.
+ Can u please bring some water for me?

## Should 
should is used to give **advise** or **something good to do**.

+ you should have to drink enough water.
+ you should try.

## Would
would is used to **request** or **offer** or **imaginary situation**.

+ would you please help me in that task?
+ i would travel more if i had money.
+ would you like some tea?
